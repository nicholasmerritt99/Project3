Welcome to mission: rescue . The victims abuse help site that will be hidden in plain site from predators so that victims have somewhere safe to go in order to get real help. Designed as a small button, to be located in the footer of any ordinary website, this will redirect users to the rescue site. The goal here is to educate victims, and get them access to shelters, hotlines, information that will provide beneficial change to their current situation.
